          Related Projects
          NOMAD is developed and used by other project. These project share common goald and value. Many team-members are active in these related projects.
          FAIRmat
          FAIRmat is a NFDI consortium that build a federated data infrastructure for solid state physics in Germany and beyond.
          • Is the main NOMAD developer.
          • Extends NOMAD beyond theory.
          • A federated data infrastructure.
          NOMAD CoE
          The NOMAD Center of Excellence is an European Union Horizon 2020 project on using exa-scale computing for computational material science.
          • Uses NOMAD to manage data.
          • Analytics on exascale hardware.
          • NOMAD for extreme scale data.
          FAIR-DI e.V.
          A multi-national NGO to prolifirate FAIR data practices in physics, chemistry, materials science, and astronomy.
          • Hosts many related events.
          • Joins related FAIR data projects.
          • Brings our ideas to other fields.
