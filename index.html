Use-cases
Publish, explore, and analyse with a single platform.
NOMAD covers the whole research cycle of shared data with a comprehensive suite of features.
cloud_upload
Publish
You can upload, share, and publish your materials science research data.
  • Uploads files as they are.
  • Manage data before publishing.
  • Publish with or without embargo.
  • Get a DOI for published datasets.

Explore
You can explore and use the data that you and others have published.
  • Rich search interface based domain specific metadata.
  • Preview entries with specialized visualisations.
  • Raw uploaded files and structured processed data.
  • All data can be downloaded and used under CC Attr 4.0.

Analyse
Automatize your work with NOMAD, programmatically access data, use machine learning and artificial intelligence.
  • All functions are available with NOMAD's APIs.
  • Machine-actionable unified data for millions of simulations and other data.
  • Ready-made tools directly run in your browser.
  • A comprehensive set of AI toolkit notebooks.
Features
What can NOMAD do for you?
You are looking for a place to share you data or use comprehensive data that others provide? You can use NOMAD to organise, analyse, share, and publish your materials science data, as well as explore, download, and analyse your colleagues data.
link
Get a DOI
NOMAD allows you to publish and archive your data for free. Assign a DOI to uploaded datasets and reference your papers.
code
All data at a glance
NOMAD extracts structured, machine-actionable data from over 50 of formats and codes. See all Your data at a glance and have it prepared for analysis.
cloud_upload
Drap'n'Drop
NOMAD organises your files as you would on your hard-drive. Upload file-by-file or zip and upload whole directory structures.
construction_outlined
Ready to use Tools
Run Jupyter notebooks and other tools directly on NOMAD. Directly access your data and publish your analysis code. No installation required.

Extendable
Add your own file formats. Create custom ELNs. Create exploration dashboards. Build and run more analysis tools.
NOMAD is the backbone of FAIRmat's federated data infrastructure
The NFDI consortium FAIRmat uses NOMAD to connect data from many research groups, institutes, and universities to build a federated data infrastructure for materials science.
NOMAD & NOMAD Oasis
Not all data is made to be published
Different NOMADs for different needs. We understand each scientific project is unique. No matter, if you just need a simple way to collaborate and publish data, or if you need full control over your data’s lifecycle, integrate data sources, and add your own tools?
NOMAD
An open and free NOMAD service for the community. You can upload and work with your data here, if your intend is to eventually publish.
  • Allows to access all published data.
  • Allows to publish data and share it with the whole community.
  • We archive your published data and you can reference it via DOI.
  • You do not need to install or host anything.
NOMAD Oasis
Your resources, your rules, your NOMAD. Organize data and collaborate locally. Keep your data on premise and control when and what is published.
  • Runs on your own resources, following your rules.
  • Allows to collaborate with everyone that you grant access.
  • You can transfer data and publish to the NOMAD from your Oasis.
  • You can extend NOMAD to your needs.
