          NOMAD
          Materials Science Data
          Organised and Shared
          NOMAD let's you manage and share your materials science data in a way that makes it truly useful to you, your group, and the community.
          used by thousands of materials scientist already
          uploaded entries 12.323.712
          covered materials 3.523.792
          uploaded raw files 59.3 TB
          Use-cases
          More than sharing files
          NOMAD processes your files to extract structured data and rich meta-data. It provides a unified way to find, access, interoperate with, and re-use (FAIR) millions of data from different codes, sources, and workflows.

          Enable your colleagues to re-use your data in a common and structured way.
          • Extract data from 50+ formats.
          • Build datasets incrementally.
          • Create customizable ELNs.
          • Get a DOI for your published data.

          FAIRmat
          Join the FAIRmat consortium and revolutionize the way research data is managed by embracing FAIR data. FAIRmat develops and operates NOMAD to build a federated data infrastructure for materials science.
          NOMAD & NOMAD Oasis
          Reaearch data management for your group
          Combine NOMAD and NOMAD Oasis to implement research data management. NOMAD Oasis gives you full control over your group's data, lets you integrate all your data sources, and lets you use your own analysis tools. Publish selected data directly from your Oasis to NOMAD for long term archival.
          NOMAD
          An open and free service for the community that lets you manage, share, and publish data.
          • Privatly manage a limited number of uploads.
          • Publish data to the whole community.
          • We archive your data and give you a DOI.
          • Access all published data.
          • You do not need to install or host anything.
          NOMAD Oasis
          Your resources, your rules, your NOMAD. Manage data and collaborate locally.
          • Runs on your own resources with your rules.
          • Control access and collaborate.
          • Manage all data in your group at all stages.
          • Publish to NOMAD from your Oasis.
          • Adapt your Oasis for your data.
