Use cases
Why use NOMAD Oasis?
There are many reasons to use NOMAD Oasis over the public NOMAD service. But, they are also connected: manage your data locally and publish selectively.
Privacy
With NOMAD Oasis you control your own data space to implement your data management policies.
  • Manage and share data without publishing.
  • Run in your network/VPN.
  • Connect to your user management.
  • Only allow a whitelist of users.
Many data
With NOMAD Oasis you use your own resources and manage as many data as you can.
  • Mount your storage solution into NOMAD.
  • Simple file-base backup.
  • You are only limited by your storage.
  • Publish selected data from your Oasis to NOMAD.
Custom needs
In your Oasis, NOMAD can be extended and customized far beyond our public NOMAD service.
  • Add your schemas and ELNs including customized processing.
  • Add support for your file formats.
  • Add your own NORTH tools.
  • Use our API to integrate with other systems.
