NOMAD & NOMAD Oasis
Not all data is made to be published
Different NOMADs for different needs. We understand each scientific project is unique. No matter, if you just need a simple way to collaborate and publish data, or if you need full control over your dataâ€™s lifecycle, integrate data sources, and add your own tools?
NOMAD
An open and free NOMAD service for the community. You can upload and work with your data here, if your intend is to eventually publish.
  • Allows to access all published data.
  • Allows to publish data and share it with the whole community.
  • We archive your published data and you can reference it via DOI.
  • You do not need to install or host anything.
NOMAD Oasis
Your resources, your rules, your NOMAD. Organize data and collaborate locally. Keep your data on premise and control when and what is published.
  • Runs on your own resources, following your rules.
  • Allows to collaborate with everyone that you grant access.
  • You can transfer data and publish to the NOMAD from your Oasis.
  • You can extend NOMAD to your needs.
