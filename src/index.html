NOMAD & NOMAD Oasis
Research data management for your group
Combine NOMAD and NOMAD Oasis to implement research data management. NOMAD Oasis gives you full control over your group's data, lets you integrate all your data sources, and allows you to run your own analysis tools. We are working on a long term archival feature that lets you publish selected data directly from your Oasis to NOMAD.
NOMAD
An open and free service that lets you manage, share, and publish data.
  • Privately manage a limited number of uploads
  • Publish data to the whole community
  • Archive your data and obtain a DOI
  • Use without any installation or hosting
  • Access all published data
NOMAD Oasis
Your resources, your rules, your NOMAD. Manage data and collaborate locally.
  • Run on your own resources with your rules
  • Control access and collaborate
  • Manage all data in your group at all stages
  • Adapt your Oasis for your data
  • Publish to NOMAD from Oasis (coming soon)
