How to cite NOMAD
If you want to mention NOMAD, e.g. in presentations, you can refer to this web-page and use the NOMAD logo. If you publish research that used NOMAD software, services, or tools cite the NOMAD JOSS paper.
  • NOMAD web-page: https://nomad-lab.eu
  • NOMAD Oasis web-page: https://nomad-lab.eu/nomad-lab/nomad-oasis.html
  • Cite the NOMAD software (JOSS): coming soon
  • Download the NOMAD logo: nomad.png, nomad.svg
  • Cite data published with NOMAD: To cite your uploaded data, please create a dataset and assign a NOMAD DOI. Refer also to our tutorial video on publishing data.
